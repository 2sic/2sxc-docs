    Roadmap of EAV and 2sxc - Vision of the Future

    The 2sxc roadmap contains the things we think are fairly important to tackle next. Since we're all working for free, there is no commitment to do this in the order you see below. And sometimes a customer will need a feature quickly - and pay for it - then it will appear sooner. You too can sponsor a feature!

    Next Priorities (EOY 2019 and 2020 Q1)

    1. Create this site (docs.2sxc.org) and migrate much of the old wiki into this
    2. Build new Admin-UI based on Angular 8
